# Dictionaries of literary terms and entities (Słowniki terminów i bytów literackich)

The collection consists of lists of literary data entities. The data is divided into two main parts - a dictionary of literary terms and a dictionary of literary entities. The latter is further divided into further subsets - authors, translators, researchers, fictional characters, actual characters, actual places, fictional places, events, institutions, literary groups, periodicals, and works.

Additional part are controlled vocabularies and ontologies of the European Literary Bibliography service.

## Contributors

**Institute of Literary Research of the Polish Academy of Sciences**, [www.ibl.waw.pl](https://ibl.waw.pl/)

## Literary terms and entities collection

Entries were extracted automatically from digitized versions of dictionaries containing literary terminology and information about people associated with literature. Another important data source was the reference directories and a subset of the National Library containing literary bibliographic records. Additional data sources were the Polish Literary Bibliography and Wikipedia.

### Dictionaries of Literary Entities

The set of literary entities consists of 154 592 entities. The collection contains entities related to the space of scientific literary discourse. The collection is divided into individual vocabularies corresponding to the types of entities that were subject to extraction. These include personal and geographical names (both real and fictional), names of institutions, names of literary groups, names of events, names of periodicals, and titles of works.

## European Literary Bibliography collection

The controlled vocabularies of the [European Literary Bibliography](https://literarybibliography.eu/) service is a collection containing multilingual data on genres and subjects found in the service's bibliographic resources. The data is presented in the Simple Knowledge Organization System (SKOS) schema and includes links to data from the National Library of Poland, National Library of the Czech Republic, General Finnish ontology (YSO), National Library of Spain and Wikidata.
The collection contains 979 entities. 

#### Access

The European Literary Bibliography dictionaries are available [here](https://github.com/CHC-Computations/Slowniki-terminow-i-bytow-literackich/tree/main/ELB%20dictionaries) in CSV and TTL formats.
The European Literary Bibliography is based on Harmonize software, the source code of which is freely available [here](https://github.com/CHC-Computations/Harmonize).
